About Us

ALBATROSS is a Kolkata-based merchandise exporting organization, serving clients across South Asia. Founded in 2003 by Mr. Samar Ganguly and Mrs. Biswarupa Ganguly, the company began by exporting CNG Bajaj Auto models to Bangladesh, and has since diversified into construction materials and their own line of wiremesh and allied products. Our core principles—integrity, honesty, and loyalty—guide every client relationship.
